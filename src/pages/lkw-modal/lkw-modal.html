The purpose of this screen is to determine when the patient was last known to be well



This screen asks for both the date and time that the patient was last known to be well. The current date and time should be filled already




Call

The time passed since the patient was last known to be well determines what kind of treatment they are eligible to receive



  • If the time passed is under four and a half hours the timer will be green and the patient will be eligible for both tPA and EVT

  • If the time passed is between 4 and a half and 6 hours the timer will be yellow and the patient will only be eligible for EVT

  • If the time passed is over 6 hours the timer will be red and the patient will be out of the usual recovery range
